Kimono Dragons 1st prototype garment

01-05-2010

At this end of quarter we made our first prototype for presentation. It was built in some concept functions such as simple animation, gesture control by accelerometer, fabric buttons, and communication via Bluetooth.

Although it was not the entire pet-raising game but at least we proved the concept is doable. We also learned the shortcoming of our original design and planed to make the reinforcement to the next generation garment.

The modifications include the garment layout, stronger connection, new sensors, discarding BT but using Xbee, adding sub display for user and etc. As well, the game itself will be constructed in next quarter. We are looking forward our second prototype could be better, stronger, and more stable. At the meanwhile, we hope the new demonstration could provide you better idea of game flow with the enhanced garment.
