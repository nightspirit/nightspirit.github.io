Orengeo POS for Win8 / WinRT

05-22-2013

This Window 8 Metro app is designed for our restaurant client to use as a POS(point and sales) system in operation. We provide all-in-one solution included 20” touchscreen device as register console, Tablets for waiter/waitress to make order, Website for online order, Report of sales history, Thermal printer, Cash drawer, and Credit card reader.

Currently we have 30+ business partners using this system for daily operation and it’s free to use.

The app is available on Windows Store

  • Front-End: Po Chen Lin (Knouckout, JQuery, WinJS, Win 8 JS APP)
  • Back-End: Joon H. Park (C#, Win 8 JS APP)
  • Design: Viktor Zheng
  • Project period: 2013-03 ~ 2013-05 (ver. 1.0)
