Orengeo Restaurant POS for iPad

02-22-2013

This app is designed for our restaurant client to use as a POS(point and sales) system in operation. The final version didn’t release due to marketing decision and we are working on the new substitution.

The coolest part is play around with card reader and remotely control thermal printer. Thanks Joon to wrap it up as phonegap plugin so I can easily use them in front-end.

  • Features: Credit Card Reader / Remote Thermal Printer / Electronic Signature
  • Front-End: Po Chen Lin (Jquery Mobile, Phonegap, Xcode)
  • Back-End: Joon H. Park (C#, Xcode, Phonegap)
  • Design: Viktor Zheng
  • Project period: 2013-01 ~ 2013-02
