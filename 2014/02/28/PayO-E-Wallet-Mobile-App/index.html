PayO - E Wallet Mobile App

02-28-2014

PayO is an e-wallet and directory mobile app for customers to easily send money and make payment within our community. It’s a hybrid mobild app using HTML5 and cordova to package as Android application.

The app is available on Google Play.

  • Front-End: Po Chen Lin (Knouckout, JQuery, Cordova)
  • Back-End: Joon H. Park (C#)
  • Design: Viktor Zheng
  • Project period: 2013-12 ~ 2014-02 (ver. 1.0)
